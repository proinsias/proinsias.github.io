> 1. Wrong Data
> 2. Missing Data
> 3. Outliers in Data
> 4. Redundancy in Data
> 5. Unbalanced Data
> 6. Lack of Variability in Data
> 7. Loss of Data
> 8. Dynamic Data
> 9. Size of Data
