You can move the cursor without arrow keys. Here is the keyboard equivalent for each.

-   Up ('previous'): `CTRL + P`
-   Down ('next'): `CTRL + N`
-   Left ('back'): `CTRL + B`
-   Right ('forward'): `CTRL + F`

Mapping `caps lock` to `CTRL` makes these combinations very accessible.
