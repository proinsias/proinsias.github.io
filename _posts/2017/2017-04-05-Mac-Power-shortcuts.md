-   `Control-Command-Power/Eject` will reboot the Mac instantly.
-   `Command-Option-Control-Power/Eject` will shut it down.
-   `Command-Shift-Q` will log off.
-   `Shift-Control-Power/Eject` will go to sleep instantly.
